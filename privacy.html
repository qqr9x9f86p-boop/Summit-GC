Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Summit General Contracting ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address submitted via our quote request form
  • Project details, budget range, and preferred timeline you include in your message
  • Communications you send us by email or phone

We may also automatically collect limited technical data when you visit our website (such as browser type and referring URL) through standard server logs. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics on this site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry
  • Schedule consultations and follow up on your project
  • Send project-related communications (estimates, schedules, invoices)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely and accessed only by Summit General Contracting staff who need it to serve you. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may link to or embed third-party services (such as Google Maps or photo hosting). Those services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the practices of third-party sites.

5. Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected (typically the duration of our business relationship) or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us at info@summitgc.example. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
